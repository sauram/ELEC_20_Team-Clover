About

R.A.F.P. or Real-time Autonomous Fire Predictor is an app built to predict
and prevent the possible incidents of bush and forest fires in an area.
The motivation for this app came from the recent mishaps caused by the
fires that originated in the Amazon Rain Forests as well as the Australian
forests which caused a huge loss to flora and fauna. With the effect of Global
warming increasing at an alarming rate, we believe that this idea,
if implemented at a large scale, can really play in important role in
saving the nature.
